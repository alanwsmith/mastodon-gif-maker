Mastodon GIF Maker

Welcome In

Hi there! I'm Alan and I'm glad you stopped by.

I was disappointed to learn Twitter is prohibiting links to other social networks. I want to let my followers know I've moved to Mastodon, but I don't want to run afoul of their rules. So, I setup this GIF maker that will let me send the message. You're welcome to use it too.

I've provided some boilerplate text. You're welcome to use it, change it up, or junk it completely and do your own thing. When you've got the words right, click the "Create it" button and it'll to do just that. (And make sure to read the notes scattered around, I just built this today and it's still clunky.)

Warning

Based off the increasing frequency of account suspensions I would not be surpised if the rules of engagement are changed again to prohibit GIFs. I'm fine with that and am using this with the idea that it could very well be the last thing I do on Twitter. I'm fine with that. I want to make sure the people I've connected with know where I've gone before I disappear completely.

Said another way: Use at your own risk

Make Your GIF

1. Pick A Background GIF

2. Update Your Text

Change this as much as you'd like and you can preview it a little further below.






3. Check Your Layout

This preview doesn't animate yet. You can get an idea from the thumbnails further above. Or, hit "Create It" as many times as you'd like.

4. And Create Your GIF!

(NOTE: the image is generated in a pop-up window which your browser will probably block the first time. Give it permission and try again and it should work. NOTE: Also, it can take a few seconds to generate.)

Save the image from the pop-up window and then you can post it on twitter. It'll look link this. (NOTE: you'll probably need to add the filename including `.gif` when you save the file. I'm working to fix that.)
